### What is GameVox?

GameVox is a privacy-first Discord alternative: voice, video, and text chat for gaming communities. Currently in open beta across Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and web.

Built by [LightSpeed Gaming LLC](https://lightspeedgaming.com), an independent company with well over a decade of experience in voice communication and game server hosting. Self-funded. No investors. No board. One team, one product.

### Privacy by Default

- No email, phone number, or ID required to sign up
- No data sales, no ads, no tracking pixels
- No voice or video recording
- Voice is end-to-end encrypted (X25519 + NaCl secretbox)
- Anonymous accounts available; your identity is your choice

### Features

- **Voice:** Opus codec, AI voice isolation, echo cancellation, spectral noise suppression, push-to-talk or voice activity, built-in soundboard and voice effects
- **Video & Screen Sharing:** Up to 1440p 60fps
- **Text:** Rich markdown (tables, task lists, footnotes, syntax highlighting, and more), @mentions, custom emoji, reactions, search
- **Community:** Forum channels, polls, events, server discovery, friends list, in-game overlay
- **Moderation:** Roles, granular permissions, audit log, AutoMod with keyword filtering, raid protection
- **Music & Video DJ:** Broadcast audio or video from any source to the server
- **GameLink:** Spatial audio for FiveM, RedM, RAGE:MP, alt:V
- **Self-Hosted:** Run GameVox on your own hardware

### Pricing

GameVox is free to use. Server upgrades are available if your community needs more capacity, but the core experience (voice, video, text, screen sharing, overlay, AI voice isolation) is free for everyone.

Pricing is per server, not per user. No stacking subscriptions. No splitting costs across members.

See [gamevox.com](https://gamevox.com) for full tier details.
